About

  • 1998

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Backed by experience in the said domain, the founders of company started activities in August 1998. Started with a small core team and with a good understanding and experience and previously good association with principals who supported in the said new venture!

  • June 1999

    A New partnership is born

    Tied up with BMG Lab Tech and which continues to be a flagship product line. Also heralded the initial focus into Life Sciences and Discovery Biology. This domain and along with Cell and Cancer Biology is a prime focus area @ Biotron Healthcare

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Service

    Having identified quite early about future growth potential within the MDx segment, initiated partnerships in this domain and continue to have innovative product line in this segment

  • 2011

    Manufacturing Program

    Initiated manufacturing program through a transfer of know how from our Japanese partner for manufacturing of Nucleic Acid Extraction kits

  • 2017

    Story Continues

    We continue to have strong working relationships with our principals. We also continue to strive and bring to our customers technology and application driven companies. We look forward to maintaining a good credibility through our quality product offering, Application and Service Support and which is well regarded in the intended domans
